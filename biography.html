Biography

Richard Li received Dean of Engineering Scholarship, when he was admitted into The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2013. He received his Bachelor degree in Computer Engineering in 2017 from HKUST. His undergraduate study was combined with Applied Mathematics, Computer Science and Electronic Engineering. He joined Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program (UROP) under supervision of Prof. Henry H. N. Lam in 2017 Summer. The work was orally presented in Asia-Oceania Mass Spectrometry Conference 2017 with Young Scientist Travel Award.

Currently, he is a M.Phil. student major in Bioengineering in HKUST. His thesis project is building an automatic vertebral edge detection tool via Deep Learning under supervision of Prof. Richard H.Y. So. Preliminary results will be orally presented in European Society for Clinical Investigation Congress (ESCI Congress) 2019 in Portugal.
